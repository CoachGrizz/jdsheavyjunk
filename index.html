FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Are you fully “heavy” capacity yet?

We’re a startup. Right now we’re best for small-to-large pickups and cleanouts while we reinvest into XL expanded capacity (truck/trailer/dump trucks).

What do you need for a photo quote?

3–5 photos, your zip code, and any access notes (stairs, long carry distance, tight parking). That’s usually enough for a fast estimate.

Do you take hazardous waste?

No. We don’t handle hazardous materials, unknown liquids, chemicals, or anything leaking. If you’re unsure, message us and we’ll advise on the right drop-off option.
